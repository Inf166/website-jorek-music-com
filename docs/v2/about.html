History:


JOREK, born 1989 in Cologne, growing up in a family of musicians. He received an early musical education in drums and percussions at a music school which pays off when he became a drummer. His fascination for electronic music was inspired by the techno movement at the end of the 90th. JOREK discovered the art of djing and producing on a Cologne based Musicschool and quickly reached a professionell level. He played withTurntables, cdjs or digital. He uses every possibility to create his individuell and original sound. On the stage and as well as in the studio, JOREK dominates his craft which is also reflected in his DJ sets. He released on Smoking Joe Records (UK), InHouse Records (Todd Terry), Motech Records (Detroit) and Supdub Records (Berlin). His latest EP "Aftermath" on Supdub Records climbed up to top 20 of Beatport Techno releases. Early 2018 he won the Bootshaus DJ Contest Techno. Lets see what comes next.
